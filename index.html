Overview of SIP

Introduction

The Sales and Install Process (SIP) at Vivint is a structured method to ensure consistency and success in delivering customer experiences.

Detailed Steps

  1. Appointment Readiness: Review customer info, discover interests, and preempt potential issues.
  2. Introduction: Win a friend, establish customer baseline, and outline appointment.
  3. Walkthrough/Design: Preempt objections, customize/build value, and verify understanding.
  4. Close: Solution/cost review, overcome objections, and start transaction.
  5. Review: Review completed work, educate customer, and benefits follow-up.

Benefits

Following the SIP ensures high-quality customer service, satisfaction, and a clear path to achieving professional goals.
