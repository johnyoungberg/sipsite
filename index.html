The Sales and Install Process

The Sales and Install Process (SIP) is crucial for delivering consistent and successful customer interactions. It helps us understand what good looks like and delivers the best customer experience.

Review customer info, discover interests, and preempt potential issues.

Win a friend, establish customer baseline, and outline the appointment.

Preempt objections, customize/build value, and verify understanding.

Solution/cost review, overcome objections, start transaction, set work expectations, and outline benefits.

Review completed work, educate the customer, and benefits follow-up.
